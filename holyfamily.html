Holy Family Catholic Church - Origin

The Holy Family Catholic Church was built to promote the establishment of happy families. Many important ceremonies are held in this church.

Holy Family Catholic Church & Santo Laurensius

Comparison

Holy Family Catholic Church:

  • The building itself is smaller but the altar is wider, and the ceilings seem taller
  • English Mass
  • A sheet of paper is provided with the lyrics and passages that are going to be read out for the day
  • New members of the Holy Family Catholic Church are greeted at the end of the mass

Santo Laurensius:

  • The building itself is wider but the altar is smaller
  • Indonesian Mass
  • Lyrics and prayers are shown on screen
  • Bibles used to be left behind for visitors but they are no longer there after the pandemic
