╰┈➤ Experiences, interesting sights, personal experiences, and comparison between the three

This page contains the workbook answers of two museums in Taiwan

  1. Liuhe Night Market
  2. Ximending Night Market
  3. Raohe Night Market

Liuhe Night Market

Experiences

We were given an hour to roam around Liuhe night market and get food. I didn't eat much though.

Interesting Sights

The streets are pretty narrow and there isn't much to see, in my opinion. But the different variants of food were interesting, and the way the street was layed out safely.

Personal Experiences

I didn't have much experience so I was panicking and in general, the situation felt really hectic.

Ximending Night Market

Experiences

We got to roam around yet again, but since it's bigger we'd have to use GPS in order not to get lost.

Interesting Sights

There were a lot of branded shops but they're a little bit cheaper compared to the stuff you'd see in Indonesia even though it isn't a HUGE difference. PLUS, second hand stores are available here and there and they have really good finds!

Personal Experiences

Again, it was hectic. But it felt a little more fulfilling-- Me and my friend just got lost over a couple of turns since we wanted to go look for clothing and stuff but it turned out to be pretty far and we did NOT have that much time left.

Raohe Night Market

Experiences

We got to wander but since Raohe night market stretches out pretty long we're warned not to go until the end!

Interesting Sights

It's narrow and crowded but somehow it doesn't feel as hectic as Ximending. It's beautiful and casual, more slow-paced, that's for sure.

Personal Experiences

I managed to get a good amount of food until I was full despite being on a low budget, the stuff there are relatively cheap. I also got a couple of trinkets.

Comparison (Between all 3 night markets)

Liuhe Night Market Ximending Night Market Raohe Night Market
Average prices Average to expensive prices Average to cheap prices
Small and doesn't stretch out much Spacious and stretches out a lot Tight but stretches out a lot and it's difficult to reach the end
Busy but not too busy, it's just a bit hasty due to the narrowness Crowded but well-managed and spacious Crowded and it's pretty difficult to weave through since it's narrow
