╰┈➤ Interesting Things We Learned & how Indonesia can Learn from Taiwan

Interesting Things We Learned

The thing we learned I was most interested in is that in Taiwan, water is used as a coolant for the nuclear power plant. However, the water isn't just wasted, it is cleaned and then reused (returned to the sea).

How Indonesia can Learn from Taiwan

I think that we can learn to be more careful with our resources, since as I see- Taiwan isn't too abundant in their natural resources. But with innovations and technology? They continue to thrive, all while preserving nature around them like the hills, forests, and oceans. We shouldn't be wasteful, despite our abundance in natural resources.
